Pancakes

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Baking powder
  • Milk and Oil
  • Eggs

procedure

  1. combine the dry ingredients.
  2. Add the wet ingredients and mix.
  3. Pour or ladle the batter onto the oiled
    griddle or pan.
  4. Cook until bubbles form,flip,and cook on the other side.

Of course,maple syrup is perfect topping for these easy pancakes.They also pair well with homemade whipped cream,blueberry compote,fresh fruit,scrambled eggs and bacon.
